Hengxiang Zhang

I am a second-year Ph.D. student in Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech), supervised by Prof. Hongxin Wei. I am grateful to collaborate with Prof. Sharon Li. Previously, I worked as an algorithm engineer at RoboSense Technology, developing LiDAR perception algorithms for autonomous driving. Prior to that, I received my M.S. degree from the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China.

My research lies in Machine Learning, including trustworthy generative AI (e.g., safety, data contamination), diffusion language models, and post-training. I am currently interested in reliably advancing research via AI, specifically in rigorous scientific experimentation, not in automating AI-driven paper generation.
