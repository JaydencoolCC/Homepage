ChineseSafe focuses on safety evaluation for large language models in Chinese scenarios. The project provides LLM safety benchmark results through the [ChineseSafe Benchmark](https://huggingface.co/spaces/SUSTech/ChineseSafe-Benchmark), along with the [ChineseSafe dateset](https://huggingface.co/datasets/SUSTech/ChineseSafe). The ChineseSafe dataset has been downloaded more than 35,000 times.
