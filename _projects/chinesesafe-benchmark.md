Our ChineseSafe focuses on safety evaluation for large language models in Chinese scenarios. The project provides LLM safety [evaluation results](https://huggingface.co/spaces/SUSTech/ChineseSafe-Benchmark), along with a publicly accessible ChineseSafe [dateset](https://huggingface.co/datasets/SUSTech/ChineseSafe). The ChineseSafe dataset has been downloaded more than 35,000 times. We hope our ChineseSafe benchmark can provides a guideline for developers and researchers to facilitate the safety of LLMs.
